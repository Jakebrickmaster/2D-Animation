2D Animation Career

What is an Animator?

An animator is someone who takes multiple images, known as frames, and spreads them out to give the illusion of a moving image. They work both with digital software and hand drawnings.

2D animators begin brainstorming with paper and pencil. Next, they use the latest editing and graphic designing softwares to finalize their designs.

Some software includes:

Adobe Flash
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Premire Pro

Adobe's programs are so far the most popular software for video editing, graphic designing, and all forms of animating
